Steve Jobs


Do you know who is Steve Jobs?


Steven Paul Jobs was an American business magnate, industrial designer, media proprietor, and investor.


  1. Born: February 24, 1955, San Francisco, California, United States
  2. Died: October 5, 2011, Palo Alto, California, United States

All about Steve Jobs


  • Steve Jobs was a charismatic pioneer of the personal computer era
  • Jobs founded Apple Inc. in 1976 and transformed the company into a world leader in telecommunications
  • Widely considered a visionary and a genius, he oversaw the launch of such revolutionary products as the iPod and the iPhone
  • Occupation: Entrepreneur industrial designer media proprietor investor
